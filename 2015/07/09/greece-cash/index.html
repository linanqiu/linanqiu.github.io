If Greece Ran on Digital Currency

2015-07-09

If the Greeks had no physical cash and only digital cash (in banks), they won’t be able to stash paper cash to wait out the conversion and drachma devaluation. Capital control would be easy and effective. Then, everyone would receive a haircut, and since the rumored haircut right now stands at anything above €8000, this would probably affect rich people disproportionately. So we end up getting a currency conversion that is hugely progressive tax from a crisis.
